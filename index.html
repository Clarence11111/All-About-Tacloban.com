About Tacloban

Tacloban is the capital and seat of government of the Eastern Visayas (Region VIII), Philippines. It is approximately 360 miles (580 km) southeast of Manila. It was the first city in Region VIII to become a "Highly Urbanized City" and is the largest city in terms of population in Eastern Visayas.
